Pitch: Website to sell my art commissions and showcase my coding ability.

Elevator pitch

Digital and traditional illustration and programming commissions - streamlined.

Longer Elevator pitch

It's a site for selling my digital commissions - sketches and digital paintings.
It will also cover my traditional paintings that I make - gouache, oils, water color, pencil, etc.
This site will also serve as a way to showcase my current coding experience, links to my current projects, etc.
The site will have my contact info, past professional illustrations, resume, a commission application form, and a way to pay tips like on patreon.
The survey form will explain costs for sketches, line art, flat colors, background, and additional characters.
It will also have links to my social media profiles.

Three Personas

This type of site can appeal to three personas:

  1. Individuals interested in contracting me for my art or code. Companies, or just regular individuals.
  2. Friends and family who are fans of my art, and want to be able to view all the art and code I've ever made, in one place.
  3. Individuals who are interested in a patreon like experience.
    Depending on how much they donate per month, get to see my works in progress, videos, as well as coding tutorials etc.

Obviously, this is just prototype content - but it could very well be a useful thing for me to have as a professional graduate this Spring.
